SOMA

is an exhibition at Hamburger Bahnhof Museum in Berlin. 'Soma' examines the mythic traditions of this Vedic elixir. One of the most important ingredient in the Soma drink is fly agaric mushroom. The living protagonists: 12 reindeer, 24 canaries, 8 mice, and 2 flies, divided half in half. On the one side some animals are not fed with hallucinogenic mushrooms; reindeer on the other side are fed with the fly agaric mushroom. After Carsten Höller takes drugged reindeer’s urine and adds it to the food of canaries and mice.
