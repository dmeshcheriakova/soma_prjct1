The myth of  the "SOMA"

Soma is a drink with healing properties known among Verdict nomads in North India that promised enlightenment and access to the divine sphere, which was commonly used in rituals. The drink's main ingredient is no longer exactly known to us; ethnomycologists and philologists generally posit the fly Amanita mushroom as the substance responsible for its effects. Also, the urine of reindeer has some specific effects as well. For example, Siberian shamans would drink the urine of reindeer, who were supposedly immune to the toxins.
